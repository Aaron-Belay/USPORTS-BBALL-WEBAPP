About Me

Welcome to U Sports Basketball Statistics, a personal project by OJ, a computer science student. This website serves as my platform to share analytics and insights into the Canadian university basketball league, commonly known as U Sports. I'm passionate about basketball and data analysis, and this project allows me to combine both interests.

Goal

My mission is to provide basketball enthusiasts, coaches, players, and fans with valuable data-driven insights and analytics to enhance their understanding and enjoyment of U Sports basketball. Through this project, I aim to promote the sport and contribute to the growth of basketball in Canadian universities.

How to Use

Explore my website to discover valuable insights into U Sports basketball. Navigate through team profiles, leaderboard stats, and standings to gain a comprehensive understanding of the league's dynamics. Whether you're a casual fan or a seasoned coach, my platform offers something for everyone.

Contact Me

If you have any questions, feedback, or inquiries, feel free to contact me. I value your input and am committed to continuously improving my platform to better serve the U Sports basketball community.

Thank you for visiting U Sports Basketball Statistics. Enjoy exploring the world of Canadian university basketball!
